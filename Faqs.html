FAQS

Please have a look
at our frequently asked
questions.

How far in advance
should I place my
cake order?

A: It is recommended to place your cake order at least 1-2 weeks
in advance to ensure availability. For custom-designed cakes
or during peak seasons, it's advisable to order even earlier.

What flavors of cake do
you offer?

A: We offer a wide range of flavors, including
classic options like vanilla, chocolate,
and red velvet, as well as gourmet flavors like salted caramel,
strawberry champagne, and lemon raspberry.
We can also accommodate specific flavor requests.
